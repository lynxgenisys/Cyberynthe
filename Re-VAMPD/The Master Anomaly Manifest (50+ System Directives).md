## **The Master Anomaly Manifest (50+ System Directives)**

Every entry follows the **Accept/Decline** logic. Failure results in a **System Penalty**.

### **Category A: Navigation & Spatial Logic**

1. **`PING_STORM`**: Visit 5 nodes in a specific, timed sequence.  
2. **`VOID_STEP`**: Navigate a 10x10 area with invisible walls.  
3. **`GRAVITY_LEAK`**: Cross moving data-platforms over a void.  
4. **`GHOST_CHASE`**: Catch a high-speed wireframe packet 3 times.  
5. **`SUDO_KEY_HUNT`**: Extract 3 key fragments from specific Sentries.  
6. **`MIRROR_NAV`**: Controls are inverted for 60 seconds. Reach a specific node.  
7. **`BLIND_RUN`**: Minimap is disabled. Reach the exit using only the **ECHO\_01** scanner.  
8. **`PATH_BREAKER`**: You must delete 10 walls using a special **`RM -RF`** script.  
9. **`SIGNAL_REPEATER`**: Stand on 3 specific "Antenna Nodes" for 10s each to boost the signal.  
10. **`DEPTH_CHARGE`**: Reach the exit portal within a strict 60-second limit.

17. **`DATA_HARVEST`**: Kill 5 enemies using only **`BIT_FLIP`** damage-over-time.  
18. **`ECHO_STRIKE`**: All kills must be performed by the **ECHO\_01** script relay.  
19. **`AGGRESSION_SYNC`**: Kill two enemies within 2 seconds of each other, 3 times.
