Welcome to the **Void Void (Floors 250–500)**. If the previous floors were a struggle against corruption, these floors are a struggle against **nothingness**. The system is no longer trying to fix the errors; it is simply letting the sectors "fall out" of the architecture.

---

## **Sector 05: The Void Void (Floors 250–500)**

### **I. Aesthetic: "The De-compiled Abyss"**

The environment transitions from a labyrinth into a series of floating, disconnected "Data Islands" connected by narrow code-bridges.

* **The Look:** Massive sections of the maze are simply... missing. You can see the infinite magenta "Under-Void" beneath the floor. Walls are semi-transparent and pulse with the heartbeat of a dying system.  
* **The Lighting:** Extreme "Chromic Aberration." The edges of your vision bleed Cyan and Magenta, making it hard to judge distances.  
* **The Atmosphere:** High-density **Bit-Rot Fog**. It hangs thick in the corridors, reducing your vision to just 5 tiles unless you use a high-gain **SCAN**.

---

### **II. Level 250 Breakthrough: Class v2.0**

Reaching Level 250 triggers the second "Permission Elevation." Your Root Class abilities evolve to handle the Eraser-tier threats.

* **The Digital Bouncer \[KINETIC\_CACHE\]:**  
  * **Effect:** Every time you hit an enemy with `HARD_RESET`, you absorb their kinetic energy. Your next script cast has its M-RAM cost reduced by 50%.  
* **The Exploit Artist \[PHASE\_SYNC\]:**  
  * **Effect:** While **STEALTH** is active, your movement speed is increased by 50%. You can move through the Void Void like a ghost, ignoring the \[MEMORY\_LEAK\] penalties.  
* **The Fabricator \[MULTI-THREAD\_ECHO\]:**  
  * **Effect:** **ECHO\_01** splits into two identical drones. You can now relay two different scripts simultaneously (e.g., `PING` and `BIT_FLIP`) to different targets.

---

### **III. Environmental Logic: \[ABYSSAL\_LOGIC\]**

* **`MEMORY_LEAK`**: The floor is so unstable that it drains your resources just to keep you "rendered." Standing still for more than 3 seconds begins to drain 2 M-RAM/sec.  
* **`NULL_ZONES`**: Large areas of the floor where scripts are disabled. You must rely on your **CLOCK\_SPEED** and physical movement to cross these zones.  
* **Safe Zone Scarcity:** \* **Guaranteed:** Every 10 levels (260, 270...) at the **Level Entrance**.  
  * **Random:** The random spawn chance drops to **10%**. At this depth, finding a random Data Pillar is a literal miracle.

---

### **IV. The Bestiary: The Eraser Processes**

The system has deployed its most aggressive cleanup tools.

1. **The `ZERO_DAY_STALKER`**:  
   * **Visual:** A flickering, humanoid glitch that is 100% invisible to the naked eye.  
   * **Behavior:** Only visible via **SCAN**. It lays "Logic Traps" that, if stepped on, lock your **CLOCK\_SPEED** to zero, making you a sitting duck.  
2. **The `VOID_WHALE`**:  
   * **Visual:** A massive, translucent entity that "swims" through the walls.  
   * **Behavior:** It doesn't attack you directly. Instead, it slowly "consumes" the maze behind you. If you take too long to find the exit, the whale will eventually consume the Exit Portal, leaving you trapped in the Void.

### **The "Void Void" Experience**

This is the "Adrenaline Spike" sector. You are constantly moving to avoid the `MEMORY_LEAK`, constantly scanning for the `STALKER`, and racing against the `VOID_WHALE` to find an exit that likely won't have a Safe Zone waiting for you.
